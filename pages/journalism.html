ABOUT FJP.

The Flint Justice Partnership at the University of Michigan aims to serve and connect with the Flint community and educate UM students about the Flint water crisis. FJP partners with multiple organizations to advance its mission.
30 percent of FJP's members are students of color and FJP was co-founded by Michal Ruprecht, Lindsay Miles, and Quinn Nolan in October of 2018.
